ABOUT US

Welcome to the Youth Entrepreneurship Program

An initiative dedicated to empowering young minds and fostering sustainable development through entrepreneurship.

Africa Governance Institute (AGI)

The Africa Governance Institute (AGI), a leading pan-African organization, has been at the forefront of promoting inclusive governance, sustainable development, and youth empowerment across the continent. At AGI, we believe that Africa’s young people are the key drivers of innovation and transformation. Our work in youth development has focused on creating opportunities, amplifying voices, and equipping young leaders with the tools they need to thrive in today’s dynamic world.

Arab Bank for Economic Development in Africa (BADEA)

This program is proudly supported by the Arab Bank for Economic Development in Africa (BADEA), whose generous financial contribution has made it possible to launch and sustain this transformative initiative. BADEA’s commitment to Africa’s development aligns seamlessly with our vision of fostering entrepreneurship as a catalyst for economic growth and social change.

Ministry of Youth Affairs

We are also honored to collaborate with the Ministry of Youth Affairs in Sierra Leone, whose unwavering dedication to youth empowerment is both inspiring and impactful. The Ministry has been instrumental in implementing innovative policies and programs to uplift the country’s young population, ensuring that they are equipped to lead Sierra Leone into a prosperous future.

Office of the First Lady

Additionally, our partnership with the Office of the First Lady brings an invaluable dimension to this program. Her Excellency, the First Lady of Sierra Leone, has been a tireless advocate for young girls, championing education, gender equality, and economic empowerment.

OUR MISSION

Together, through this program, we aim to create a robust platform that nurtures entrepreneurial spirit, fosters innovation, and equips young Sierra Leoneans with the resources they need to turn their ideas into impactful businesses.
